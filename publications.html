Conferences and Presentations

I have participated in a range of conferences and workshops, sharing research on reading, memory, and cognitive processes. My contributions include oral presentations, poster sessions, and logistical support.

  • HSP, 38th Annual Conference on Human Sentence Processing (University of Maryland, USA, 2025)
  • CCD Annual Workshop (Macquarie University, Australia) – Poster presentation on multi-character processing interventions for dyslexia
  • The Australian Eye-Tracking Conference (Macquarie University, Australia) – Assisted with registration, IT support, and logistics
  • Problems of Language: From the Perspective of Young Scientists (Russian Academy of Sciences, Moscow) – Presented an experimental study on working memory and text reading
  • CECILS3 (Central European Conference in Linguistics, Hungary) – Poster presentation on how working memory capacity affects information perception
  • XVI International Conference of Students-Philologists (St Petersburg, Russia) – Plenary session presentation on individual differences in reading and working memory
