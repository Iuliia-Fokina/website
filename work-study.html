Education

PhD

In August 2022 I enrolled in the PhD program at the Department of Psychology and Neuroscience at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill.

MRes

I completed a Master of Research in Cognitive Science at Macquarie University, Faculty of Human Sciences, Department of Cognitive Science. My research thesis, "Multi-Character Processing Intervention in an English-Speaking Child with Dyslexia," focused on developing targeted interventions for reading difficulties. The thesis can be accessed here.

MA

I earned a Master's degree in Linguistics from Saint Petersburg State University, Faculty of Fine Arts and Languages, Department of General Linguistics. My research thesis, "The Role of Working Memory in Perception of Relevant Information During Reading (An Experimental Study in Russian)," examined cognitive processes involved in reading comprehension and information retention.

BA

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Linguistics from Saint Petersburg State University, Faculty of Fine Arts and Languages, Department of Romanic Languages. My graduation project, "Simile and its Functions in Romanian Fiction," explored the role of figurative language in Romanian literature.
